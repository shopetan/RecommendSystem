1844252000 Rubens Barrichello: In the Spirit of Senna And the Shadow of Schumacher Johnny Herbert (はしがき), David Tremayne (著) Haynes Pub Group 3380 2005-11-10 F1 オートレース レーシング・カー When he graduated to Formula 1 in 1993 Rubens Barrichello looked a potential starr. By 1996 - still grieving the death of his mentor Ayrton Senna - Rubens seemed a 'might have been', struggling with an uncompetitive Jordan. However in 1999, driving for Jackie Stewart, he proved a mature performer and Ferrari beckoned. The Brazilian is a rare mix - tough enough to be Michael Schumacher's fastest-ever team-mate yet so gentle off-track that he is the most popular driver in the paddock. This is his rollercoaster story.
